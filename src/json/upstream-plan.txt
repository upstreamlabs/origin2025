核心理念
 Core Philosophy
Upstream Ignite 
以开源精神为根，以公共价值为核，探索可持续发展的新范式。
商业化在这里并非终点，而是维护开源生态活力与信任的支撑力量。
Rooted in the spirit of open source, centered on public value, Upstream Ignite explores a new paradigm of sustainable development. Commercialization here is not an end, but a means to sustain vitality and trust within the open ecosystem.
🌱 
项目定位与目标
 Positioning & Objectives
Upstream Ignite 
是一个以
 
开源生态建设与可持续发展
 
为核心的学习与实践平台，致力于帮助参与者理解开源的文化、制度与社区逻辑，并通过真实项目实践，探索开源在社会与技术层面的长期影响力。
主要目标
 | Objectives:
1. 
普及开源文化与治理理念
 Spread open-source culture and governance principles.
2. 
培育具备社区运营、传播与可持续能力的生态型人才
 Cultivate ecosystem leaders capable of community and sustainability management.
3. 
联合政府、产业与社区资源，促进开源公共产品长期发展
 Build collaboration across government, industry, and community to sustain open-source public goods.
